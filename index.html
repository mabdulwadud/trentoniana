The Phonebooth Project

About This Project

This project aims to highlight one of the many artifacts found in the Trenotoniana exhibit at the Trenton Free Public Library. It's creation is made possible by The College of New Jersey, Prof. Warren Buckleitner, and of course, the Trenton Free Public Library. The item focused on for this project is a vintage phonebooth. These were very common from the early-to-mid 20th century. Since its retirement, it has sat unused for decades. Our hope is that the phonebooth can be brought to life, where anyone can listen in to calls from the past that could have very well taken place in this booth.
